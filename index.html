About SpeedSlice

SpeedSlice is the ultimate app for finding local pizza joints that are open and delivering to you. whether you’re having an office pizza party, having a night in, or stumbling home from the bar at 2am, we’re going to give you insanely convenient web and mobile apps to find pizza joints that’ll deliver what you want right away.

We’re currently in beta mode. This means our restaurant coverage is limited, but we’re constantly working on expanding. Right now, we’ve got the best coverage in our hometown of Boston, (and Cambridge, too!)

Because we’re so new (and scrappy), we had to make choices about what features to add, so here goes:

  • all pizzas you order will be large pizzas, defined however the place you order from defines ‘large’
  • The toppings we offer are limited right now, but we’ve got the most popular ones covered :)
  • We’re going to be adding features and toppings as we go!
  • All peppers are green peppers
  • All olives are green olives

That’s it for now! Contact us anytime with questions, concerns or ideas: support@speedslice.com. We’d love to hear from you!

SpeedSlice Support

Issues with your Order

If you’ve got an issue with your order, we’re here to help. We want you to have a great pizza experience.

Your first (and best) move is to call the restaurant that is fulfilling your order. Their contact information is on the confirmation email we sent after you placed your order. If you can’t find this email, be sure to check your spam folder.

If you can’t get in touch with the restaurant, please email us at support@speedslice.com and we’ll do our best to remedy the problem. You can also call us at 617-752-1129. We may not be able to pick up right away, but we will return your call and figure things out as soon as possible.

I got a Message that told me my Order Wasn’t Confirmed, What’s up with that?

Sometimes in the restaurant industry things go unexpectedly wrong: you have to close early for the day or you are so swamped you can’t take orders. Or a giant pizza monster devours your delivery car. Really, it could be anything.

When a restaurant doesn’t confirm the order that we sent over within 10 minutes, we tell you, and cancel the order. If your order wasn’t confirmed, you won’t be charged for that pizza. You may see a pending charge on your credit card, but it won’t be charged.

Feel free to place another order, as usual - we want you to get that pizza!

Issues with the App

If the app is giving you trouble, or there’s something you’d like to add to it, let us know, we love feedback and want to learn from it. Let us know what browser or phone you’re on when you email support@speedslice.com to give us a heads up. Thank you!

I Own a Restaurant and I’d like to be on SpeedSlice

Awesome! Email support@speedslice.com with subject “Restaurant” and we’ll get you on board - our onboarding process is simple and more-or-less instant. You could be accepting orders as early as tonight! Or fill out the form here

If you have a favorite restaurant that you want on SpeedSlice, we’d be happy to talk with them. Send us an email at support@speedslice.com and we’ll do our best to make it happen!

I’ve got a Business Proposal, who do I talk to?

If you’d like to talk to our head of business development, email chris@speedslice.com and tell us what’s up. We’re very open to new partnerships.
